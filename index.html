About Me

I'm Rudi, an 18-year-old professional Actor and Voice Actor based in Blackpool, Lancashire. I have a year of experience in acting and voice work, with a versatile style that ranges from vibrant and energetic to dark and gritty. I strive to bring every character to life with emotion and depth, using both my voice and physicality to craft compelling performances.

I'm currently studying Theatre and Performance Practice at the University of Salford, developing skills in acting, directing, devising, and stagecraft. I also regularly train at The Actors Workroom, refining my technique through script reading, stage performance, and collaborative sessions.
